What is the unemployment rate calculator?
The unemployment rate calculator is a tool that calculates the unemployment rate of a country.
How to calculate unemployment rate?
To calculate unemployment rate you should use the following formula:
unemployment rate = (unemployed people / (employed people + unemployed people)) * 100
Why use unemployment rate calculator?
The unemployment rate calculator is used in order to measure the unemployment rate of a country.
